About Us

Swift Hygiene and safety Limited was incorporated in the republc of kenya in the year 2015.

We are equipped with modern and latest hygiene an safety equipment to ensure safety of your staff,efficiency and high hygiene standards.

Our Objectives

To Provide services that consistently meet the needs and expectation of our clients.To achieve this we will srive to:

  • Providing services with state of the art of methodology.
  • Providing solutions to suit customer needs in the most economical way without compromising on the required quality.
  • To some extent if need be, provide documentation support.
  • Adhere to quality assurance standards and work completion as per schedule.

    • Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide professional services to our clients,with specialized solutions that suits their unique needs with highly skilled professionalism,administration and skill supported by technical practice experience.

    Our Values

    Responsibilities

    Acting with integrity towards our staff,our customers the community and the environment.

    Caring

    A duty of care for our staff,customers and environment.

    Excellence

    To always look to provide the best quality experience with regards to our standards and our customer service.

    Respect

    Taking time to understand and value each person and respecting there choices.

    Integrity

    To act with honesty,openness and deliver on our promise.

    Innovation

    To be industrial leaders.

    Our Vision

    Our vission is to become the leading provider of hygiene, safety and other support services to home and work places within the country and beyond.We strive to build lifelong relationships with our clients resulting in a better quality life.We will be an employer of choice,fostering a culture that values dedication,integrity and continous improvements.
